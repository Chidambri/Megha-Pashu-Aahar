About Us

Megha Pashu Aahar was established in 2004 with a vision to support farmers by providing high-quality cattle feed supplements that promote better animal health and productivity. Since our beginning, we have been committed to delivering reliable and nutritionally balanced feed solutions for livestock.

We specialize in the development and supply of cattle feed supplements designed to improve digestion, strength, milk yield, and overall well-being of cattle. Our products are carefully formulated using quality ingredients and modern nutritional practices to ensure consistent results.

At Megha Pashu Aahar, we believe that healthy cattle are the foundation of successful and sustainable farming. Our mission is to build long-term trust with farmers by offering safe, effective, and affordable feed supplements that meet the evolving needs of the livestock industry.
